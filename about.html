Dept. of Electronics and Communication Engineering

Electronics and Communication Engineering department one of the first departments at TIST has stood firm in its objective to impart quality education and training
at undergraduate and postgraduate levels in various areas of Electronics & Communication Engineering.
The post graduate courses are M.Tech in VLSI and Embedded System and M.Tech in Electronics (Wireless Technology) which is introduced for the first time in Kerala
with a typical annual intake of 18 for each course. Department includes the Toc H Agilent Advanced Communication Lab facility worth Rs 1.2 Crores.
The branch is powered by Industry experienced faculty in the areas of VLSI, DSP, Mobile & Wireless Communication, Data Communication & Microwave Electronics,
who brings the right blend of academic and research expertise and industrial experience from national and international organizations like DRDO, AT & T, BAARC etc.
Alumni of the Department have good experiences to share being placed in reputed organizations like Wipro (VLSI), Tata Elxsi, CTS, Nokia, Idea, Infosys and Sasken etc.
